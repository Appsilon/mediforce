Why now

The infrastructure for AI in pharma doesn't exist yet

AI agents are ready

Large language models can do real cognitive work: review documents, extract insights, draft reports, flag inconsistencies. The capability is here.

AI is ready. Process isn't.

Teams want to use AI today. But regulated work needs structure, oversight, and audit trails that most AI tools don't provide.

No standard exists

Every organization reinvents how to integrate AI into clinical processes. Mediforce is building the open standard for human-agent collaboration in pharma.

How it works

You define the process. The platform handles compliance.

For each step in a workflow, you decide who does the work — human, AI agent, or both. Agents operate at one of four autonomy levels, and the platform enforces the rules.

Level What the agent does Human involvement
Observer Watches and reports None — informational only
Advisor Suggests actions Human decides and acts
Drafter Does work, submits for approval Human approves or sends back
Executor Acts autonomously Human reviews after the fact
Built for regulated environments

Compliance built into every layer

Audit trail

Every action is recorded — who did what, when, and why. Full traceability from agent decision to human approval.

Accountability

Clear record of who is responsible for each step. Humans and agents have defined roles — no ambiguity about who made a decision.

Open source

Inspect every line of code. No vendor lock-in, no black boxes. Deploy on your infrastructure, audit the logic, contribute back.
