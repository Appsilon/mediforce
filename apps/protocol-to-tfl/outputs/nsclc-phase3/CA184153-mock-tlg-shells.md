### Key Considerations

1. **mWHO criteria (not RECIST):** This study uses modified WHO criteria for tumor response, which are bidimensional (sum of products of perpendicular diameters). Reference thresholds differ from RECIST: PR = >=50% decrease in SPD, PD = >=25% increase. All response tables and figures use mWHO terminology.

2. **NCI CTCAE v3.0 (not v4/v5):** Protocol specifies CTCAE v3.0 for AE grading, which is older than current standards. Footnotes should reflect this.

3. **Chinese subpopulation:** ~85% of subjects are from China. Pre-specified Chinese subset analyses for OS and PFS are secondary endpoints. Separate TLGs mirror the overall efficacy analyses.

4. **Phased ipilimumab design:** Blinded therapy starts at Cycle 3 (Week 7). ~24% of subjects expected to never receive blinded therapy. This creates the distinction between All Randomized and Rand+Blinded populations.

5. **Double-blind design:** All tables use "as randomized" grouping for efficacy and "as treated" for safety. No unblinding summary needed during the study.

### Items Requiring Clarification Before Finalization

- **Stratified vs. unstratified log-rank test:** Confirm that the primary test is unstratified (per Section 8.4.2.1) despite Synopsis language suggesting stratification.
- **BORR/DCR/DOR for overall population:** Clarify whether these are formal secondary endpoints for the overall Rand+Blinded population (not just Chinese subset).
- **Full standalone SAP:** The posted SAP is only the protocol's Section 8. A complete SAP with ADaM derivation rules, full TLG listing, and detailed missing data strategy should be obtained.
- **irAE/imAR MedDRA term lists:** The exact predefined lists for immune-related AE categorization should be confirmed from the full SAP or sponsor algorithm documentation.
- **Duration of Response (DOR):** Consider adding DOR tables if response analysis is extended beyond BORR for the overall population.

### Potential Additions

1. **Chinese subset safety tables** — Beyond the overall AE summary (T-14.3.1.1c), consider adding Chinese-specific irAE and lab tables if regulatory submission to NMPA requires separate safety characterization.
2. **Swimmer plot** — Could be informative for visualizing individual treatment duration, response onset, and maintenance phase entry.
3. **Exposure-response analysis** — If ipilimumab PK data are analyzed within this study (vs. pooled analysis), consider adding exposure-response figures.
